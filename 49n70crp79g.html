In order to test the null hypothesis, we can apply a Welch two-sample t-test to determine whether the means of two gene expressions of populations are statistically different. The null hypothesis is:
    H0 : µ1 = µ2
where
  • μ1 is the mean expression for gene tc4 in ALL patients
  • μ2 is the mean expression for gene tc4 in AML patients
